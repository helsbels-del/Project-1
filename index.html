"Above all, learn to breath correctly." - Joseph Pilates

Here at the Moving Cat Studios, we base our work on the same three principles that Joseph Pilates, the first person to use these techniques, used.


Breath, whole-body health and whole-body commitment; with the whole-body incompassing mind, body and spirit. It is in the honouring of the Pilates Principles that the depth of work is achieved.


Head to our History page find out more about this amazing man.


We opened our first studio in central London back in 2000 and have since opened 3 more studios around the UK, in Manchester, Newcastle, and Edinburgh.
